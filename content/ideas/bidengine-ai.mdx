## The Problem: $700B on the Table, and Small Businesses Can't Reach It

The US federal government spends over **$700 billion annually** on contracts. By law, 23% of those dollars are supposed to go to small businesses. In practice, the system is so opaque and complex that most small businesses never bid — and those that do often lose to incumbents who have dedicated proposal teams.

Finding relevant opportunities requires monitoring SAM.gov (the federal procurement portal), dozens of state procurement sites, and agency-specific solicitation boards. The average Request for Proposal (RFP) is 50–200 pages of dense requirements. Writing a compliant response takes 40–80 hours of skilled labor. For a 10-person company, that is an enormous bet on an uncertain outcome.

**BidEngine AI** automates the entire pipeline: discovery, qualification, proposal generation, and submission — making government contracting accessible to businesses that currently cannot afford to play.

## Market Analysis

### TAM / SAM / SOM

- **TAM**: $12B — total addressable market for GovTech procurement tools globally
- **SAM**: $3.8B — US-focused SaaS tools for contract discovery, proposal writing, and compliance
- **SOM**: $380M — small and mid-size businesses (10–500 employees) actively registered on SAM.gov

### Why Now?

1. **AI proposal generation**: GPT-4-class models can draft compliant proposal sections that previously required specialized consultants ($150–$300/hr). The quality gap has closed dramatically.
2. **Government digitization push**: Federal mandates are pushing agencies toward digital-first procurement, creating structured data where only PDFs existed before.
3. **Set-aside expansion**: New programs for veteran-owned, minority-owned, and women-owned businesses are increasing the addressable pool of eligible bidders.
4. **Remote work normalization**: Post-COVID, service delivery is no longer location-dependent, opening federal contracts to companies nationwide.

## The Product

### Core Features

**1. Smart Discovery**
AI-powered monitoring of SAM.gov, state procurement portals, and agency solicitation boards. Machine learning models score each opportunity based on the company's capabilities, past performance, certifications, and win probability.

**2. Bid/No-Bid Analyzer**
For each opportunity, the platform generates a structured analysis: estimated effort, competition level, margin potential, and strategic fit. A clear recommendation: bid, monitor, or skip.

**3. AI Proposal Generator**
Upload a solicitation and the platform:
- Extracts all requirements and evaluation criteria
- Maps them to your company's capability statements and past performance
- Generates a compliant first draft organized per the solicitation's instructions
- Flags missing information and compliance gaps

**4. Compliance Checker**
Before submission, the platform validates the proposal against FAR (Federal Acquisition Regulation) requirements, formatting rules, page limits, and mandatory certifications. Catches the administrative errors that disqualify 15–20% of proposals.

**5. Team Collaboration**
Assign proposal sections to subject matter experts. Track progress against the submission deadline. Version control for proposal documents. Review and approval workflows.

**6. Win/Loss Analytics**
After submission, track outcomes. The AI analyzes debrief data and competitor patterns to improve future proposals. Build an institutional knowledge base that compounds over time.

### Technical Architecture

- **Data Pipeline**: Scrapers and APIs for SAM.gov, USASpending.gov, FPDS, state portals — normalized into a unified opportunity schema
- **ML Models**: Opportunity scoring (XGBoost on historical award data), win probability estimation, competitor identification
- **LLM Pipeline**: Fine-tuned models for proposal section generation, trained on publicly available winning proposals (FOIA-accessible)
- **Backend**: Python/FastAPI microservices, Celery for async document processing
- **Frontend**: React with real-time collaboration (CRDT-based) for multi-author proposal editing
- **Storage**: PostgreSQL, Elasticsearch for opportunity search, S3 for documents

BidEngine AI turns a 60-hour manual process into a 6-hour AI-assisted workflow. The compounding advantage comes from win/loss data — every proposal outcome makes the platform smarter. And the success-based pricing model means revenue scales with customer success.
